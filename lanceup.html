Lance Up

Lance Up is a 2D video game developed in Gamermaker Studio

As a Lancer, the main character is to venture through the levels defending themselves from the enemy lancers and picking up various drops. The Lancer must ustilize their drops to solve various puzzles and defeat level bosses in order to progress further.
