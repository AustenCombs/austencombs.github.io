TEGR Virtual Tours

The TEGR Virtual Tours touring system involved the redevelopment of a web-based application that allowed students and visitors to gather information about various aspects of the Dennis and Carol Troesh Engineering building via the use of QR codes.

The Gordon and Jill Bourns College of Engineering (CoE) virtual touring system aims to assist in the growth and knowledge of their students and visitors alike. TEGR Tours provides a fun and self-sufficient way to navigate the building without the need to schedule an appointment ahead of time. Thereby, allowing guests to explore at their leisure and learn more about what goes on in the College of Engineering on their own time.
